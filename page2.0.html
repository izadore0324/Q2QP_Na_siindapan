Initiatives

Through comprehensive digital preservation initiatives, the society establishes and maintains an extensive archive of rare recordings, employing advanced restoration projects to rescue and digitize deteriorating performances. Educational outreach programs engage schools and institutions globally, fostering a deeper understanding of the historical significance of classical and traditional music. The organization's annual international concert series features renowned musicians, showcasing archival treasures and promoting cross-cultural appreciation. Embracing technological innovation, T.U.N.E.S collaborates with experts to develop tools that make old music accessible, while community engagement initiatives nurture a global network of music enthusiasts, ensuring the continued celebration and preservation of our rich musical heritage.
