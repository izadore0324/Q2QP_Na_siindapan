Achievements

  • Digital Preservation Archive
    • T.U.N.E.S has successfully created a comprehensive digital archive, preserving a vast collection of rare and endangered musical recordings spanning various genres and historical periods.
  • Historical Restoration Projects
    • The organization has spearheaded ambitious restoration projects, collaborating with skilled audio engineers and music historians to revive deteriorating or damaged recordings.
  • Educational Outreach Programs
    • In an effort to share their passion for old music with the wider community, the organization has developed educational programs that engage schools, universities, and cultural institutions.
  • International Concert Series
    • T.U.N.E.S organizes and sponsors an annual international concert series, featuring renowned musicians dedicated to the performance of old and classical music.
