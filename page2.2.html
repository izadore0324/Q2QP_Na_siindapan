Activities and Participation

Melodia Resonance Society engages in a variety of activities and collaborations to fulfill its mission of preserving and promoting vintage music. The organization actively curates and maintains a digital archive, safeguarding rare and endangered recordings through meticulous preservation initiatives.


Looking ahead, T.U.N.E.S envisions expanding its commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility through innovative initiatives. The organization plans to implement eco-friendly practices in its archival and preservation efforts, exploring energy-efficient technologies and sustainable storage solutions to minimize its ecological footprint.


T.U.N.E.S actively seeks collaboration with like-minded associations and organizations to amplify its impact on the preservation of vintage music and promote shared values. The organization plans to participate in international conferences and symposiums hosted by music preservation and cultural heritage associations, fostering a collaborative exchange of knowledge and best practices.
