Involvements

T.U.N.E.S has successfully established fruitful collaborations with government entities, achieving notable milestones in its efforts to preserve and promote vintage music. The organization actively engages with cultural preservation initiatives supported by government agencies, contributing expertise and resources to the development and implementation of policies that prioritize the safeguarding of historical musical heritage.


Through its advocacy efforts, T.U.N.E.S has played a key role in influencing government funding and support for cultural preservation programs. The organization has successfully secured grants and sponsorships, enabling it to expand its archival and restoration projects, digitization efforts, and educational outreach initiatives.
